  • Detailed cygwin installation, emacs installation (2005/02/16) !!New!!
  • The following briefly explains how to install & run cygwin on Windows:
    • download base_cygwin.zip , .cshrc
      • unzip base_cygwin.zip, in the directory of cgywin, double click Setup
      • then "Next", and choose "Install from Local Directory", "Next"
      • "Next", "Next", then wait until the window "Select Packages"
      • move cursor to "All Default", click on that Default, then wait for a while until every default becomes Install , then "Next"
      • now it is installing the package. It may require several minutes. Then we are done!
    • after installation, you can copy .cshrc to c:/cygwin/home/XXXX where XXXX is your login account (e.g. Administrator)
    • Now click on your desktop to launch cygwin
    • after the cygwin windows pop out, you may keyin "tcsh" then press enter
    • now you can do whatever you want in cygwin
