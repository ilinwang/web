Class Participation (5%)

  1. 學期中除第一堂課所發出的「修課學生背景調查」外(佔1%)，將不定期發出另外2份問卷 或 多份簽名(共佔4%)

  4. 若課後1星期內尚沒有來填寫者，該次一律以0%計算
  5. 本5%為額外加分用

Paper Report (15% = 3% + 7% + 5%)

2 papers preview report (by 2016/03/28) 3%

  1. The 2 academic papers that you choose must be written in English (unless you can persuade me for exception). These papers must be related with Network Optimization, and at least one of them is communication network related. If you are not sure, come to ask me.
  2. Submit a short report (both paper and html) for these 2 papers in which you should
    1. Briefly introduce these 2 papers, explain why you choose them to study (such as why it is related to the course, and what you want to learn from the paper ..etc)
    2. Choose 1 of the 2 to be the midterm paper report, whether the other one will be used or not will be announced later in the semester, depending on how the course goes.
    3. Check detailed format

Midterm paper report (written report by 2016/03/28, present after 2016/04/25) 12%

  1. Each student has to submit a paper report (at most 15 pages, both in paper and in doc/pdf) before the class of 2016/03/28
  2. A 15-30-min presentation will be held after 2016/04/25.
    A presentation pdf/ppt file should be turn in by that course, too.
  3. Check detailed format

Quiz/Exam (45% = 2*10% + 25%)

General rules

  1. DO NOT try to cheat, or you will not only get 0%, but also get other penalties.
  2. Unless you have a very good reason/excuse, a no show means 0%. Make-up exams will only be made for some very special cases. So, please inform the lecturer much earlier if somehow you can not take the quiz/exam at that specific time.

2 quizzes (2016/03/28, 2016/05/16) 2*10%

  1. The quiz will be held on Thursdays about 2-hr written quiz, and may be 1 more hr programming quiz or oral quiz, so you had better be on-time.

Final exam (2016/06/20) 25%

  1. The exam will be held in the final week. Be careful about the time slot announced by the school later.
