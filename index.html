Reesearch Interests

I am interested in solving optimization problems, developing efficient and effective algorithms to solve easy/difficult/fundamental/challenging optimization problems. In general, problems that are easy/fundamental in fact are also "difficult" or "challenging" since it will take much more efforts to have some new breakthroughs or contributions. (Yet I am still enjoying doing it, that's why I have done something in shortest paths and max-flows.)

The research topics I have done, been doing, or will do cover the following applications & techniques:

  • Network Optimization
  • Logistics, Supply Chain Management
  • Bioinformatics
  • Personal Navigation System in Metropolitan Area
  • Network Reliability
  • Semiconductor Manufacturing
  • Data Mining
  • Project Management
  • Pavement Optimization
  • Telecommunication

For details, go to Research page.
Awards

By 2023 fall (Oct.), I have advised 65 master students (doing master theses), and 9 groups of undergraduate students (doing projects). My master students have won 62 awards, and undergraduate students have won 13 awards in master thesis, projects, or paper competitions hosted by OR Society of Taiwan (ORSTW), Chinese Institute of Industrial Engineering (CIIE), DHL, Chinese Management Association, and the Ministry of Science and Technology.

Besides, I also have received the best poster award four times in the OR subdivision of the National Science Council (NSC, later known as MOST) IE division in 2009, 2016, 2018, and 2022. I have lead a team "NCKU" with 2-3 master or undergraduate students to win the problem-solving competition held by the Railway Application Section of INFORMS for 7 times, in 2010 (honorable mention), 2011 (3rd place), 2013 (honorable mention), 2014 (2nd place), 2016 (2nd place), 2017 (1st place), and 2019 (2nd place).
